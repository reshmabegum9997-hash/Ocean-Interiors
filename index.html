About Us

Ocean Interiors is your trusted partner for UPVC solutions, serving Tamil Nadu with premium quality doors and windows. Based in Parangipettai, we specialize in crafting high-quality products that combine style, durability, and functionality.

Our installations are resistant to weather, sound, and time, ensuring your home remains beautiful and secure for years to come.

Our Services

  • UPVC Window Installation: Sliding, Casement, Tilt & Turn, and Fixed Windows.
  • UPVC Door Installation: Sliding, Casement, French, and Balcony Doors.
  • Mosquito Mesh Solutions: SS Black Electro Coated and Retractable Systems.
  • Customized Fabrication: Tailored designs with color options.
  • Commercial & Residential: Serving Homes, Offices, Hotels, and Hospitals.
